The Journal · Lab-grown, explained

Four letters. One price tag.

Cut, colour, clarity, carat — what each one means and which one matters most.

Every diamond — lab-grown or mined — is graded on four criteria. Understanding them takes minutes. Using them correctly saves thousands. Note: in 2025, GIA replaced the full 4Cs grading system for lab-grown diamonds with two simplified categories — Premium and Standard. IGI continues to use the complete 4Cs. Every Velonna stone carries an IGI certificate, so you always get the full picture.

Cut — the most important C

Cut is not the shape of the diamond — it is the quality of how it was faceted. A well-cut diamond bounces light from facet to facet and throws it back through the top of the stone. A poorly cut diamond leaks light through the bottom and looks dull. IGI grades cut from Excellent to Poor. Always choose Excellent. It is the single biggest driver of how beautiful a diamond looks in real life.

Colour

Diamonds are graded from D (completely colourless) to Z (visible yellow or brown tint). The difference between D and G is almost impossible to see with the naked eye once the stone is set. G–I is the sweet spot — near-colourless, not premium-priced. Unless you are buying a very large stone where colour becomes more visible, there is no reason to pay for D–F.

Clarity

Clarity measures the number, size, and position of internal characteristics (inclusions) and surface characteristics (blemishes), assessed at 10× magnification. The scale runs from Flawless (FL) to Included (I3). VS1–VS2 or SI1 is the sweet spot for most buyers — eye-clean to the naked eye, without the premium price of VVS or Flawless grades. For larger stones (above 1.5ct), consider stepping up to VVS2, as inclusions become more visible at size.

Carat

Carat is weight, not size. One carat equals 0.2 grams. Price increases exponentially at round-number thresholds — 0.5ct, 1.0ct, 1.5ct, 2.0ct. A 0.95ct and a 1.0ct stone look virtually identical. Choosing just below a round number saves significantly with no visible difference.

The sweet spot

The Velonna sweet spot

G colour · VS2 clarity · Excellent cut · just under a round carat weight.

This combination delivers maximum brilliance, eye-clean appearance, and the best value for your budget.
